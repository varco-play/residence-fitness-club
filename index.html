About Us

Where Ambition
Meets Excellence

Residence Fitness Club was built for a singular purpose: to provide a training environment worthy of your ambitions. We didn't compromise on space, equipment, instruction, or atmosphere — because you shouldn't have to.

From our Olympic-grade swimming pool to our state-of-the-art strength and conditioning floor, every detail is curated to support serious results. Whether you're beginning your fitness journey or pushing the boundaries of performance, our expert coaching team is here to guide you every step of the way.
